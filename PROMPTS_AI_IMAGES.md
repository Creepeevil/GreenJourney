# Prompt tạo ảnh AI cho website “Tái sinh trang giấy”

Dùng các prompt này để tạo thêm ảnh cho website. Nên dùng tỉ lệ 16:9 cho banner, 4:3 cho card, 1:1 cho avatar/icon.

## 1. Hero banner

```txt
Soft eco fantasy illustration for a Vietnamese nonprofit website, young students and volunteers recycling old paper into new notebooks, forest hair silhouette, floating paper sheets, glowing leaves, warm cream and emerald palette, hopeful, friendly, educational, semi-realistic digital illustration, no text, no watermark.
```

## 2. Workshop tái chế giấy

```txt
Children and volunteers in a rural classroom making recycled notebooks from old paper, warm sunlight, paper textures, green shirts, friendly community atmosphere, soft illustration, pastel teal, cream, leaf motifs, no text, no watermark.
```

## 3. Tài liệu vệ sinh kinh nguyệt

```txt
Age-appropriate menstrual hygiene education illustration for middle school students, friendly notebook infographic style, warm pink and cream colors, sanitary pad icon, calendar, handwashing, supportive tone, not graphic, no text, no watermark.
```

## 4. Tài liệu hành động khí hậu

```txt
Climate action education illustration, recycled paper turning into a green forest and school notebook, small students planting trees, paper scraps flying like leaves, teal green cream palette, magical yet clean website illustration, no text, no watermark.
```

## 5. Cộng đồng kể chuyện xanh

```txt
Friendly social media story card image, Vietnamese teenagers holding recycled notebooks with handmade covers, smiling naturally, rural school background, soft sunlight, green and cream color palette, hopeful community feeling, no text, no watermark.
```

## 6. Quyên góp giấy/vở cũ

```txt
Warm illustration of hands giving old notebooks and clean paper stacks to a volunteer collection box, leaves and paper textures, nonprofit donation feeling, clean modern website style, emerald green, beige, soft gold, no text, no watermark.
```

## 7. Avatar người dùng

```txt
Friendly Vietnamese student avatar, warm smile, green scarf or leaf pin, soft illustrated style, circular profile portrait, cream background, gentle lighting, no text, no watermark.
```

## Negative prompt chung

```txt
watermark, logo, random text, distorted hands, scary mood, poverty exploitation, sad children, dark horror style, explicit medical detail, graphic content, low resolution, blurry, cluttered typography
```
